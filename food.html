Lontong Balap

Soupy Food

The origin of the name Lontong Balap comes from the sellers who always run when carrying their heavy merchandise so that it looks like they are racing. Lontong Balap itself is one of Surabaya's specialties that serves fried tofu, lentho or green bean cakes, boiled bean sprouts, then doused with a sweet and savory sauce.

Lontong Kupang

Soupy Food

Lontong Kupang is also one of the typical Surabaya foods that you must try. Kupang itself is a kind of small shellfish that is boiled then given a splash of savory broth with lime juice and chili paste. So don't miss it to taste the delicious taste of this typical Surabaya food.

Pecel Semanggi

Snack

This typical Surabaya food serves steamed clover, a type of nail plant which is then mixed with papaya leaves and bean sprouts and then doused with peanut sauce which is savory, sweet, and spicy.

Tahu Tek

Heavy Meal

Tahu Tek comes from the sound "tek" when the seller uses cooking utensils to fry tofu or cut ingredients. Generally served with sliced rice cakes, potatoes, fried eggs, and fried tofu. To make it even more delicious, tofu tek will be doused with petis, sprouts, and equipped with prawn crackers mashed with peanuts.

Sate Kelapa

Heavy Meal

Sate Klopo is one of the typical foods of Surabaya which is located on Jalan Mayor Mustajab which was formerly called Jalan Ondomohen. Sate Klopo is made from meat covered with grated coconut and spices before being grilled. No wonder this satay becomes more savory and tender.
